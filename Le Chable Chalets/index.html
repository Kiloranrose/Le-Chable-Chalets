Chalet Luda

Traditional Swiss Chalet in the heart of Le Chable

Le Chable is a pretty village with a good community spirit. There are several shops, including 2 supermarkets, 2 pharmacies and a bakery with coffee shop, within walking distance of the chalet. There are also several restaurants to suit all budgets, again all within walking distance.

In the winter Le Chable offers excellent access to skiing and other winter sports. It is also possible to sample the nightlife of Verbier as a bus runs after the lift has shut. The last bus down from Verbier is at 00:30 and after that you can go with a 15 minute taxi ride. In the summer there are many exciting activities such as mountain biking, hiking, riding and paragliding as well as more sedate activities such as swimming, walking, golf and tennis. It is also a perfect base for taking on the challenging Haute Route.

Chalet Luda

A charming and sympathetically renovated ski chalet in the quiet village of Villette, Le Chable. The chalet is a 5 minutes walk from the bubble (ski lift) which connects with the world famous ski resort of Verbier.

The chalet has a large modern kitchen with wonderful views of the mountains. There is a large dining room table which seats 8 comfortably but can be extended twice to seat up to 12 people depending on the size of your group. The spacious sitting room has a wood burning cast iron stove which creates a very cosy atmosphere and has 2 large 3 seater sofas. The chalet has 4 bedrooms. Bedroom 1 and 2 are very large and each have a little sitting area. Three bedrooms are ensuite and there is a further family bathroom with washer/dryer.
